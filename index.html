About: Allamm is a web developer, digital archivist, and musician from Glasgow, Scotland, and has worked on countless sites in the digital giving sector including customisations for Alzheimers Research, G.O.S.H, Mental Health UK, Irish Cancer Society and many more. Once a 'scholar', Allamm has an MA in digital media and information studies and a MSc in mobile web development. Before this project, Allamm has contributed music for video art installations, developed archives for arts organisations, and played guitar in a rock band. As a musician (electronic, techno, experimental, rock), Allamm's music comprises of synthy, deep and expressive techno compositions aimed to surprise and present the listener with personal experiences. Next release 'Altruistica' is out on April 19th 2024.
